Contractor Onboarding Process

1
Initial Email Sent 7 October
Email is sent to both contractor admins and contractor employees requesting profile setup.
↓
2
Link Distribution
Contractor employees forward setup links to their subcontractors.
↓
3
Subcontractor Admin Setup
Subcontractor admins accept the link and create their company profile.
↓
4
Worker Profile Creation
Subcontractor admins create individual profiles for their workers.
↓
5
Induction & Compliance
Workers complete inductions and upload compliance documents.
⚠ Note: This stage may trigger an influx of support requests.
